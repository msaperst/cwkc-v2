ONE TEAM. ONE COMMUNITY. ONE FAMILY. ONE COMMONWEALTH. ONE CUP.

Welcome to the Commonwealth Kiddush Cup - a thirteen-year tradition of competition between Hillel at the University of Virginia and Hillel at Virginia Tech. This fundraising battle sees alumni from both schools face off to raise more money for their school's Hillel - providing essential support for the many programs each Hillel runs for students throughout the year.

Thank you for giving generously to support our Hillels. Each donation makes a direct impact on how students at both schools are able to deepen their connections to Judaism.
